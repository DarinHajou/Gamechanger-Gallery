Gamechanger Gallery
==================

Gamechanger Gallery is a web-based sports legends gallery that offers a unique and immersive experience to sports enthusiasts. It showcases a collection of digital images featuring sports legends, transformed using cutting-edge AI technology and the Midjourney Discord bot. The project aims to combine the worlds of sports, art, and technology to provide a captivating and innovative platform for exploring sports legends and their transformative journey through AI-powered image manipulation techniques.

Users can browse through the transformed images of sports legends categorized by various artistic styles, including cubism, surrealism, pop art, and more. This allows users to explore different visual interpretations of these iconic figures and gain a fresh perspective on their impact and legacy.

Features
--------

- Explore: Dive into the world of sports legends and discover captivating digital artworks that showcase their achievements and iconic moments. The Explore section allows users to navigate through different artworks and learn more about the athletes.

- Transformations: Experience the power of metamorphosis with the Transformation section. Witness the artistic representations of sports legends through various artistic styles and transformations, offering a fresh perspective on their impact and legacy.

- Athlete Details: Delve deeper into the lives and careers of sports legends with the Athlete Details section. Get access to comprehensive information about each athlete, including their biography, birthdate, nationality, and more.

- Search: Easily search for specific athletes or styles using the search functionality. Enter keywords related to athletes or artistic styles to quickly find relevant information and artworks.

Dependencies
------------

The Gamechanger Gallery project relies on the following dependencies:

- Font Awesome: An iconic font and CSS toolkit for adding visually appealing icons.
- Sanity: A headless content management system (CMS) used for fetching and managing data related to athletes and artworks.

Please ensure you have an internet connection to properly load the required dependencies.
